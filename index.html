Sport-N-Fun We offer something for everyone

About

Come check out our family fun attractions today. Teams, groups & special events are welcome! Hourly rental is available.

Sport-N-Fun is cash only, with an ATM provided on the premises.

Go Karts

Choose from 3 Quality 18 Hole Putting Courses. Players are distracted by wildlife statues overlooking their every shot while obstacles, hills, bank shots and more confront the best players. Each course offers challenging levels from easy to hard - ALL promising an awesome time! Come play with your friends, family or group today!

Putt-N-Fun

Hey Batter, Batter, Batter… Sport-n-Fun boasts 7 batting cages, offering both Softball & Hardball. Choose from slow pitch to hurling pitches of up to 85 miles per hour - faster than a cheetah! Bats and Helmets are provided, though you may choose to bring your own. Come play today and enjoy the FUN while improving your batting skills! Walk-ins are WELCOME! TEAMS are WELCOME.

Batting
Cages

Come experience the high flying excitement! This activity affords EVERYONE the opportunity to experience an extreme sport. The Bungee Trampoline allows you to perform acrobatics - a great confidence booster and learning tool too! While children race to be the first in line, parents eagerly wait their turn to bounce too! Get harnessed in while you bounce high in the sky, flipping your way to fitness while having a FUN-tastic time!.

Trampoline

Those that have raced around Sport-n-Fun’s Go Kart track may identify themselves as drivers at the NASCAR, Grand Prix or Indy 500 as they race down the straight-aways, twists and turns, maneuvering around other Formula “K” Sprint Cars while attempting to set their Best Records!
